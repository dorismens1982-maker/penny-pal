# Penny Pal Video Demo Guide 🎬

This guide is designed for a video editor/compiler to create a smooth, high-energy demonstration of the Penny Pal application for a live show or promotional reel.

## Scene 1: The First Impression
**Action**: Open the app and land on the Splash Screen/Welcome page.
**Visual**: The Penny Pal logo appears with a subtle animation.
**Key Message**: "Meet Penny Pal—Ghana's Smart Finance Companion."

---

## Scene 2: Quick Onboarding
**Action**: Click the "Get Started" or "Sign In" button.
**Visual**: Show the Google/Email login screen. A quick "Login Success" message or transition to the Dashboard.
**Key Message**: "Zero friction. Instant access."

---

## Scene 3: Manual Income Logging
**Action**: 
1. Navigate to the **Manage** tab.
2. Click the floating **(+)** button.
3. Select **Income**.
4. Type "Salary" and "5000".
5. Click **Save**.
**Visual**: The "Net Balance" card glows and updates its value in real-time.
**Key Message**: "Log every cedi with a tap."

---

## Scene 4: Manual Expense Logging
**Action**: 
1. Click the **(+)** button again.
2. Select **Expense**.
3. Choose "Groceries 🛍️".
4. Enter "250".
5. Click **Save**.
**Visual**: The "Expenses" card increments and the 7-day chart adjusts its peaks.
**Key Message**: "Stay on top of your spending."

---

## Scene 5: The Hero Feature—AI Voice Logging 🎙️✨
**Action**:
1. Click the **Mic Icon** on the Dashboard.
2. Hold to record: *"I just spent 150 cedis on fuel at Goil."*
3. Release.
4. **Visual**: The "AI is thinking... 🧠" loader appears.
5. **Visual**: The Transaction Modal pops up with "150", "Expense", "Transportation 🚗", and "Fuel at Goil" **automatically filled**.
6. Click **Confirm**.
**Key Message**: "Just talk. We'll handle the rest."

---

## Scene 6: Beautiful Insights
**Action**: Scroll through the **Overview** dashboard and tap the **Analytics** tab.
**Visual**: Smooth animations of the Recharts area charts and category breakdown.
**Key Message**: "Visualize your wealth, achieve your goals."

---

## Scene 7: Wrap Up
**Action**: Back to the home view with the "Voice Announcement Banner" visible.
**Visual**: Fade to the Penny Pal logo with the tagline: *"Smart Finance. Better Vibes."*
**Key Message**: "Penny Pal. Download now on mpp.com."

---

### 🎨 Video Style Tips:
- **Pacing**: Fast cuts for logging; slower, smooth pans for Analytics.
- **Audio**: Upbeat, modern afro-pop or clean tech-ambient background music.
- **Overlays**: Use text callouts for numbers (e.g., "+5000", "-250") to make them pop.
